Welcome to my implementation of Oauth 2.0

Purpose of the page:

The purpose is to be able to go through the Oauth flow so that our app is authorized to access certain parts of your Google Email profile information and obtain a JWT.

How do we do this?

By clicking on the "Google Sign In" button, you will be redirected to Google's Email sign in page and then redirected back to our user info tab. There you will see your first name, last name, and your user ID value of that was used to secure the original redirect.

You will be able to see your new JWT generated.
